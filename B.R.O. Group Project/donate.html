Donate to the orchestra!

With a philanthropic gift of $200 or more (we'll leave that up to you) you can become and Orchestra Benefactor and be placed on our Benefactor roll for twelve months. This roll will be published in each conert program and on our website for twelve months. As a benefactor you will also receive two complimentary tickets to our next concert, be placed on the mailing list for our newsletter and will be a guest at our annual Season's Launch function.
